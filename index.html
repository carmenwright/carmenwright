How I Got Here

I'm based in Las Vegas by way of Southern California, Northern Nevada, and upstate New York. As an avid reader, I graduated with an MA in English from the University of Rochester. After I decided not to pursue a PhD, I examined what I wanted to pursue and came across Skillcrush, signued up for their Web Designer Blueprint, and found myself loving what I learned.

Currently, I'm enrolled in the Front-End Developer Blueprint with Skillcrush.
